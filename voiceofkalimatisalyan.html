About Us

Voice of Kalimati Salyan is a public voice and news platform dedicated to sharing local news, public concerns, tourism promotion, development activities, and important updates from Kalimati Rural Municipality, Salyan.


Our mission is to provide transparent information, raise public voices, promote tourism, and connect local people through digital media platforms.
